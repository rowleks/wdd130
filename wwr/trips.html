Family Rapids Adventure

Perfect for beginners and families, this half-day trip takes you through gentle rapids and stunning scenery. Our guides ensure a safe, fun experience for all ages. Includes snacks and all equipment.

  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Minimum Age: 6

Full Day Whitewater Challenge

For thrill-seekers! Tackle Class III-IV rapids on this action-packed full-day adventure. Enjoy a riverside lunch and breathtaking views. No experience needed, but be ready for excitement!

  • Duration: 7 hours
  • Difficulty: Moderate/Challenging
  • Minimum Age: 12

Multi-Day Wilderness Expedition

Immerse yourself in nature on this 3-day, 2-night rafting and camping trip. Experience remote rapids, starry nights, and delicious campfire meals. All gear and meals included.

  • Duration: 3 days, 2 nights
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Minimum Age: 14
